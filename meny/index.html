— a trail for every thirst —

We grade our drinks using a trail system. Each cocktail is rated by intensity, strength, and how brave you're feeling right now. Don't worry — if things go sideways, our rescue team (also known as the bar staff) is standing by.

The Trail System
E
Beginner Trail
For the curious, cautious, or simply thirsty.Light, approachable and friendly. Sweet, fresh and citrus forward. Smooth sailing — no special skills required.
E
Intermediate Trail
For confident walkers with decent balance.A step up in flavour and complexity. You'll still enjoy the view, but you might need to pay attention.
E
Advanced Trail
For experienced hikers only.Spirit forward and unapologetic. Less citrus, more backbone. These drinks don't hold your hand.
E
Expert Trail
For legends, lunatics, and people who read the ingredients twice.Bold, boozy and intense. Endurance and solid footwear is required.
